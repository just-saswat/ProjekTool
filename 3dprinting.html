Materials We Offer

  1. PLA (Polylactic Acid):
    • Eco-friendly and easy to print, PLA is perfect for prototypes and decorative items.
    • Low warping, vibrant colors.
  2. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene):
    • Strong and heat-resistant, ABS is ideal for functional and mechanical parts.
    • High strength, durable.
  3. PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol):
    • Tough and flexible, PETG is great for functional parts and containers.
    • Moisture-resistant, durable.
  4. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane):
    • Flexible and rubber-like, TPU is suitable for wearables and shock-absorbing parts.
    • Elastic, impact-resistant.
  5. ASA (Acrylonitrile Styrene Acrylate):
    • UV-resistant and durable, ASA is perfect for outdoor and weather-resistant applications.
    • Weather-resistant, tough.
  6. Nylon :
    • Strong and flexible, Nylon is ideal for mechanical parts and gears.
    • High strength, abrasion-resistant.
  7. CF (Carbon Fiber) :
    • Lightweight and strong, Carbon Fiber is used for high-performance parts.
    • Rigid, high-strength.
  8. Resin :
    • High-resolution and smooth, Resin is perfect for detailed models and prototypes.
    • Precision, smooth finish.
